Yuni SUSANTI

A research scientist specializing in NLP/IR/Semantic Web and related AI technologies. Previously worked at the AI Lab in Fujitsu (Japan) on knowledge extraction and neuro-symbolic AI for causal discovery. Prior to this, I completed my PhD at Tokyo Institute of Technology, Japan.
